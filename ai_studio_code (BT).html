The Heritage Challenge

Traditional solid walls (pre-1919) were designed to breathe. When modern, non-permeable insulation is applied, moisture becomes trapped within the masonry, leading to structural damp, mould, and decaying timber.

The BreatheTherm System

Our mineral-based system mimics the capillary action of traditional lime, allowing moisture to transition through the wall while providing industry-leading thermal performance.

  • Fully Vapour-Open: No more damp or interstitial condensation.
  • A1 Non-Combustible: The highest standard of fire safety.
  • Dimensionally Stable: Won't warp, shrink, or degrade over time.
  • Heritage Approved: Specified for Grade I and II listed properties.
