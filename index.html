The Challenge

Traditional Walls Need to Breathe.

Most modern insulation acts like a plastic wrap, trapping moisture within Georgian and Victorian masonry. This leads to structural damp, mould, and the decay of historic fabric.

The Vapour Crisis:

  • Standard PIR boards cause interstitial condensation.
  • Synthetic renders trap rising damp.
