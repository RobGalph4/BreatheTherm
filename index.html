The Problem

Traditional Walls
Need to Breathe.

Most modern insulation acts like a plastic wrap, trapping moisture within Georgian and Victorian masonry. This leads to structural damp, mould, and the irreversible decay of historic fabric.

The UK's 5.9 million pre-1919 homes are at risk — not from the cold, but from the wrong solution.

Interstitial Condensation

Standard PIR boards create cold bridges that cause moisture to condense inside the wall structure — invisible, devastating damage.

Trapped Rising Damp

Synthetic renders seal the wall face, forcing ground moisture upward and accelerating the decay of lime mortar and masonry.

Loss of Heritage Character

Bulky insulation systems alter window reveals, mouldings and proportions — the very features that define a Georgian or Victorian home.

The Solution

The Five-Element
"Plug & Play" System

A modular mineral system — mix and match components to solve specific thermal challenges, from thin window reveals to full solid wall build-ups.

01

Aerogel Boosters

NASA-grade silica aerogel blanket delivers exceptional thermal resistance in just millimetres — preserving every centimetre of your reveals.

02

Perlite Base Render

Expanded volcanic rock base coat provides thermal mass and capillary activity — actively managing moisture rather than blocking it.

03

Breathable Finish Coat

Hydrophobic mineral skin repels external driving rain while remaining fully vapour-open — protection without suffocation.

04

Hygrothermal Membrane

Intelligent variable-resistance membrane adapts to seasonal conditions, opening in summer to dry and closing in winter to protect.

05

Monitoring Integration

Optional embedded sensor suite provides live hygrothermal data — confidence for architects, certainty for Conservation Officers.
