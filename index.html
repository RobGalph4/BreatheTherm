The Challenge

Traditional Walls Need to Breathe.

Most modern insulation acts like a plastic wrap, trapping moisture within Georgian and Victorian masonry. This leads to structural damp, mould, and the decay of historic fabric.

The Vapour Crisis:

  • ✕ Standard PIR boards cause interstitial condensation.
  • ✕ Synthetic renders trap rising damp.

The Solution

A Vapour-Open Mineral Shield.

BreatheTherm is a fully breathable, mineral-based system that works with your building's natural capillary action, not against it.

  • + Vapour Permeable: Allows moisture to escape safely.
  • + A1 Fire Rated: Pure mineral composition, zero fire risk.
  • + Thermal Excellence: Drastic reduction in heat loss.
