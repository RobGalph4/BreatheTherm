Traditional buildings behave differently.

Solid masonry walls were designed to absorb and release moisture naturally. Modern impermeable insulation traps that movement, leading to condensation, mould and long-term fabric decay.

BreatheTherm is engineered specifically for traditional construction — allowing safe vapour migration while delivering meaningful energy savings.
