The Challenge

Most insulation is designed for modern buildings.
Yours is not modern.

Pre-1919 solid wall buildings are fundamentally different in their construction logic. They were designed to absorb and release moisture — a dynamic thermal envelope that modern vapour-barrier insulation can catastrophically disrupt.

Understanding Moisture in Solid Wall Construction
Conventional insulation on solid walls

Vapour barriers trap moisture against masonry, leading to interstitial condensation at the insulation–wall interface.

Mould, timber decay and structural damage result — particularly around lintels, sole plates and embedded timbers.

Historic fabric is irreversibly damaged. Listed building consent complications. Warranties void.

BreatheTherm vapour-open mineral system

Mineral-based insulation with high vapour diffusion resistance factor (μ) compatible with traditional masonry — moisture moves freely through the system.

Interstitial condensation risk eliminated. Embedded timbers and structural fabric protected. Historic lime finishes preserved.

Fully reversible installation. WTA-compliant. Compatible with Listing requirements and Conservation Area policies.

The BreatheTherm System

A mineral insulation system built on the logic of traditional construction.

We don't fight the physics of old buildings. We work with them. BreatheTherm is a complete vapour-open mineral system — from substrate preparation through to breathable finish plaster — validated by hygrothermal modelling for UK heritage conditions.
